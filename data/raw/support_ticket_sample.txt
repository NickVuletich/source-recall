category: support ticket

Ticket: Retrieval returns unrelated sources
Status: open

problem:
When the dataset is small or top_k is too high, SourceRecall may retrieve sources that are only loosely related to the question.

example:
A question about Sarah's action items may retrieve business notes correctly, but also include project or fitness notes as lower-ranked sources.

possible fixes:
- rename output from Sources to Retrieved Sources
- allow top_k to be configured from the command line
- improve chunking strategy
- add filtering by document category in the future
